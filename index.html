About Us

The East African Youth Medical Charity Program is a nonprofit organization dedicated to delivering essential medical services, health education, and preventive care to underserved youth across East Africa.

Founded in 2021, we conduct medical camps, operate mobile clinics, train community health workers, and respond to emergencies — all with a focus on sustainable impact.

Our Programs

Mobile Clinics

Bringing medical care directly to remote villages.

Youth Health Education

Training youth in hygiene, nutrition, and mental wellness.

Emergency Response

Quick relief during outbreaks, floods, and crises.

Medical Camps

Offering free health screenings and specialist consultations in underserved communities.
