About Us

The East African Youth Medical Charity Program is a nonprofit organization dedicated to delivering essential medical services, health education, and preventive care to underserved youth across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi.

Founded in 2020, we operate mobile clinics, train community health workers, and respond to emergencies — all with a focus on long-term, sustainable impact.

Our Programs

Mobile Clinics

Bringing doctors, vaccines, and medicine directly to remote villages.

Youth Health Education

Workshops on nutrition, hygiene, mental health, and disease prevention.

Emergency Response

Rapid aid during outbreaks, floods, and refugee crises.

Medical camps

Medical camps in underserved rural areas, providing free health screenings, vaccinations, essential medications, and specialist consultations to over 500 patients per event, while promoting community health education and follow-up care.
